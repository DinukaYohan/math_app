About Our Project

MathAI helps students and teachers generate curriculum-aware math questions by grade, language, country, topic, and model. Our aim is to make practice fast, consistent, and accessible.

Built with precision and adaptability, MathAI ensures that every question generated aligns with real-world education standards. The system empowers teachers to save time on manual preparation, while giving students unlimited access to customized exercises suited to their curriculum and progress level.

The project blends advanced AI models with user-friendly design to make learning mathematics interactive and engaging — bridging the gap between technology and classroom learning.
