About This Project

A simple tool to generate curriculum-aware math questions.

Purpose

This website helps students and teachers quickly create math practice questions. You can choose the country, language, grade, and the AI model to tailor questions to your needs.

How it works

  1. Enter a short prompt (e.g., “fractions word problems”).
  2. Select Country, Language, Grade, and Model.
  3. Click the arrow to generate questions in the output box.

Privacy

We only use the text you provide to produce questions. Do not include personal or sensitive information in prompts. Your session data is cleared when you start a new chat.
